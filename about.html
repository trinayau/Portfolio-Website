I first became interested in coding after being inspired by a close friend who built a career in the technology industry in just two years, from a position of not knowing how to code at all. After dedicating my evenings to learning how to code, I found myself enjoying the process and committing increasing time to it. I love the logical nature of coding and found a new satisfaction in being able to apply this to solving problems and creating visual outputs that are unique to me.

I started looking for resources that would help me learn to code. I found that SheCodes' online coding workshops offered me a self-paced introduction to front-end development using HTML5, CSS, Javascript ES6 and the opportunity to code real-life projects. I used what I learnt to code the projects that you can see here.

After talking to peers in the tech industry and researching online, I realised that a career in tech would also combine aspects I enjoy from my work in the pharmaceutical industry, such as the creative and problem-solving aspects, with what I want in the future: to work collaboratively with peers in a challenging and innovative environment.

I am currently training with futureproof as a Tech Trainee, completing a 13-week tech academy. I am learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Redux as well as SQL and NoSQL, Python (Flask and Django), Node and Express. I have also learnt agile ways of working in a tech team.
